Frequently Asked Questions

How do the code challenges work?

Each day, we post a new coding challenge related to Solana development. You'll have a limited time to fix the bug or complete the code. If your solution passes all tests, you'll automatically receive your reward to your connected wallet.

What rewards can I earn?

Rewards include CodeCoin tokens, exclusive NFTs, and leaderboard rankings. The more challenges you complete, the more rewards you'll earn. Special challenges may offer bigger rewards, and consistent participation unlocks bonus rewards.

How do I connect my wallet?

Click the "Connect Wallet" button and select your preferred Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.). Make sure you're on the Solana network to receive your rewards. If you don't have a Solana wallet yet, we recommend installing Phantom Wallet.

When do new challenges appear?

New challenges are released every 24 hours. The countdown timer shows the time remaining for the current challenge. Make sure to complete each challenge before the timer expires to earn rewards.
